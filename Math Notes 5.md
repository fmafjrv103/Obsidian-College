# General Form to Standard
- $f(x)=-2x^2+4x+5$
- $f(x)=ax^3+bx+c$

###### Standard Form
$f(x)=a(x-h)^2+k$

### Step 1
Identify the coefficient $a$
### Step 2
Identify $b$ and $c$

### Step 3
Find $h$
$h=\frac{-b}{2a}$

$h=\frac{-4}{2(-2)}=\frac{-4}{-4}=1$

### Step 4
Find $k$
$f(h)=k$

$\begin{align} f(1)&=-2(1)^2+4(1)+5 \\ &=-2+4+5 \\ &=7 \\ 7&=k \end{align}$
### Step 5
Plug into Standard Form

$\boxed{f(x)=-2(x-1)^2+7}$
